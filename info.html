Duck Hunt is an exciting shooting simulation where the player ventures out with their faithful canine companion to hunt ducks. With a first-person perspective, the player is immersed in the marshland scenery as their dog skillfully flushes the ducks out of the vegetation, sending them into flight.

Using the mouse, the player has full control over their aim and shoots at the flying ducks. Only one duck appears at a time, and the objective is to hit it before it can escape. The player has three shots and a limited time of 10 seconds to bring down the duck. Precision is key, as each round of Duck Hunt allows only a limited number of errors to progress successfully.

As the player advances through the various levels, the speed of the ducks increases, making the game progressively more challenging. At the tenth level, the difficulty reaches a new level with the appearance of two ducks simultaneously. Now, the player must hit both without fail; missing two shots results in the guaranteed escape of one of the ducks. As the levels progress, the ducks become even more agile, heightening the challenge and excitement of the game.

Each duck shot down earns the player points, while failing to hit a duck does not result in a loss of points. However, the goal is to achieve the necessary accuracy to accumulate points and progress in the game.

The "Game Over" occurs when the player fails to hit the stipulated number of ducks for each round, challenging them to improve their skills to face new challenges.
